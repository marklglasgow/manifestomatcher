    About Manifesto Match
    A non-partisan tool for Scottish voters
    What it is

    Manifesto Match is an independent, non-partisan tool helping Scottish voters understand which parties best reflect their own policy priorities ahead of the 2026 Scottish Parliament election.

    It is not voting advice. It is a starting point for your own research.

    What it is not

    We do not endorse any political party. We are not affiliated with any party, campaign, or political organisation. No party has paid to be included or scored in any particular way.

    Party scores involve judgement, and reasonable people may disagree. Our methodology is fully transparent — see the Methodology page.

    How the data works

    Each party has a data file that contains their scores on every topic. Until a party publishes its 2026 manifesto, scores are estimated from existing public policy positions and clearly labelled as estimated. When a manifesto is published, the data file is updated and the site reflects the new scores automatically on next load.

    All data files are openly available in the site's GitHub repository. If you believe a score is incorrect or would like to suggest a correction, please use the Contact page.
